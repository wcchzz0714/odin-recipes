Spicy Hot Chicken Legs

Description

This is just like spicy chicken wings, only less work. Drumsticks cook up nicely in the slow cooker! A quick meal that is super easy and oh so yummy! For crispier chicken, bake in a 400-degree F oven for the final 30 minutes.

Ingredients (5 servings)

  • 10 chicken drumsticks
  • .83 (5 ounce) bottle hot red pepper sauce
  • 3 tablespoons and 1 teaspoons butter, cubed
  • ⅖ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ⅖ teaspoon onion powder
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 ¼ cups blue cheese salad dressing

Steps

  1. Place drumsticks in a slow cooker, and sprinkle evenly with pieces of butter. Pour hot sauce over the chicken, then season with garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
  2. Cover, and cook on High for 3 hours, or until tender. Serve chicken legs with blue cheese dressing on the side.
